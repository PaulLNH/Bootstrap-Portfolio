About Me

My name is Paul Laird, I’m a husband to a phenomenal wife, father of three awesome children, the sole financial provider for my family, and I just quit my corporate job. This might seem absurd but hear me out.

I’m also an outdoors enthusiast, entrepreneur, and web developer. I love being outdoors and staying active, something my desk job would never allow. I find businesses fascinating! I’ve created a couple small businesses myself and it’s something I truly enjoy. Being creative and implementing a variety of business systems is not something I had the freedom to do locked in my office. It was time to break up with that oppressive relationship I called a job. I didn’t walk away from the paycheck, but rather I opened the door to my own creativity.

You see, to me success is measured by the lives I impact, the experiences I share and the memories that are created. That is why I quit my job, to help people just like you.

If you have an interesting project you'd like to talk about, let me know!

Together, we will build something great.
